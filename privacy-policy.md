# Privacy Policy — ZionBrowser

**Last updated: March 29, 2026**

## Data Collection

ZionBrowser collects **ZERO personal data**. All browsing happens locally on your device.

## Permissions

- **INTERNET**: Required to fetch web pages
- **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE**: Required to check connectivity

## Data Storage

- **Cookies**: Stored locally on your device only
- **Cache**: Temporary page cache stored locally, auto-cleared after 5 minutes
- **History**: Browsing history stored locally, never transmitted

## Third Parties

ZionBrowser does **not** share any data with third parties. No analytics, no tracking, no ads.

## Search

When you search, queries are sent to DuckDuckGo (a privacy-respecting search engine). ZionBrowser does not log or transmit your queries.
